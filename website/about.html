about

imdown is an app to make it easier to schedule events with friends. we're working on streamlining the process between proposing a hangout and getting an event on your calendar.


the way it works:

  • get on the app and join a squad with your friends
  • propose an event to your friends
  • if enough friends rsvp that they are down for the event, the event gets scheduled automatically on your calendar.

that's it! no fuss. no back and forth. just having fun and hanging out.


we only use your data to make scheduling easier. we only collect what you explicitly provide and are focused solely on making event planning with friends less of a hassle.
